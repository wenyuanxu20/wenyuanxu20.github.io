About Me

My name is Wenyuan Xu. I finished my undergraduate study and received my bachelor's degree at the University of Liverpool in 2017, the major is Electrical and Electronic Engineering(Electronic Eng&Communications). During my undergraduate study, I also visited Montana State University in the United States in the summer vacation of 2016.


Then I received offers from the University of Southampton, the University of Birmingham, the University of Warwick, and the University of Glasgow. I selected and attended the M.S. winter and spring course (visiting scholar) in the University of Warwick as research assistant. During the 6 months in Warwick, the winter/spring term helped me to acquire the knowledge of coding theory, digital signal processing, data mining, optical communication, image processing.


In 2019, I received the master project offer from XJTLU, the major is MSc.multimedia telecommunications from department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering. During the 18 months study in XJTLU, I enjoyed my study in the area of deep learning and computer vision, which includes: image and video processing, deep learning and computer vision, and advanced pattern recognition. In 2021, I received my Master's degree from UoL with distinction.

After completing my studies, I joined the IT/gaming industry for 3 years. Subsequently, I was recommended to become a lecturer in communications/computing at the University of the West of Scottland. I was assigned to work at a Sino-foreign cooperative (Scottland academy) college in Wuxi.
