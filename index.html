About Me

As a 2+2 project student from XJTLU(Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University), I finished my undergraduate study and got my bachelor's degreeat the University of Liverpool in 2017, the major is Electrical and Electronic Engineering(Electronic Eng&Communications).


Then I received offers from the University of Southampton, the University of Birmingham, the University of Warwick, and the University of Glasgow. I selected and attended the M.S. winter and spring course (visiting scholar) in the University of Warwick because I wished to study in the area of artificial intelligence/machine learning. During the 6 months in Warwick, the winter/spring term helped me to acquire the knowledge of coding theory, digital signal processing, data mining, optical communication, image processing.


In 2019, I received the master project offer from XJTLU, the major is MSc. multimedia telecommunications from department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering. During the 18 months study in XJTLU, I enjoyed my study in the area of deep learning and computer vision, which includes: image and video processing, deep learning and computer vision, and advanced pattern recognition. In 2021, I received my Master’s degree from UoL with distinction.
